Financial planning is the process of managing your money to achieve personal economic satisfaction.
A good financial plan includes budgeting, saving, investing, and preparing for unexpected expenses.

Key principles of financial planning include:
1. Setting clear financial goals.
2. Maintaining an emergency fund.
3. Diversifying investments to reduce risk.
4. Monitoring and adjusting your plan as life circumstances change.

Long-term financial stability requires discipline and consistent review of spending habits.
